PSG College of
Technology

PSG College of Technology is an autonomous, government aided, private engineering college in Coimbatore, India. It is affiliated with Anna University. It is affiliated to PSG and Sons Charities Trust.

Moulding Future
Engineers

Among engineering colleges in India, PSG College of Technology ranked 30 among engineering colleges by India Today in 2020 and 23 among all engineering colleges in India by Outlook India in 2019.The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) ranked it 49th among engineering institutes in 2020 and 85th overall.
